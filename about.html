Our Story

Authentic Lebanese Flavor, Imported with Tradition

Taste of Phoenicia was born from a deep love for Lebanese cuisine and a desire to share its authentic flavors with the American market. Founded in 2020, our family-owned business is rooted in the rich culinary heritage of Lebanon—a land where food is more than sustenance; it's a way of life, a celebration of community, and a bridge between generations.

Based in Chicago, Illinois, we serve retailers, restaurants, and distributors across the United States. But our heart remains connected to North Lebanon, where we source every product with care and intention.

Our Lebanese Roots

Lebanon's northern region is renowned for its olive groves, fertile valleys, and generations of farmers who have perfected the art of traditional food production. It is here that we build lasting relationships with local producers—families who have been making tahini, pickling vegetables, and pressing olive oil using methods passed down for centuries.

Working with Local Farmers

We believe in supporting the people behind our products. By sourcing directly from North Lebanese farmers and producers, we ensure that our customers receive the highest quality goods while helping sustain traditional agricultural practices. Every jar, bottle, and package represents not just a product, but a partnership built on trust and mutual respect.

Our Commitment

Authenticity and quality are not just words to us—they are the foundation of everything we do. All our olives are hand-picked and cold-pressed following international quality standards to preserve their nutrients, aroma, flavor, and color. We never compromise on the natural ingredients or traditional methods that make Lebanese food so special.
